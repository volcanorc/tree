# The Lineage Archive

This is a visual archive of my family history- a clearer way to see who belongs to which branch, how they are connected, and where each family line continues.

It’s still in its early stage, so it doesn’t include every ancestor i have. Some information is still missing, and honestly, I don’t care enough to dig deep right now. Maybe future me will.

The archive shows the people currently recorded, their basic information, family connections, and whether they are still alive or have passed away. explore the map, open individual profiles, and follow each branch without trying to understand a giant list of names.

There is also a separate archive for the pets we’ve owned, because why not.

Some details may be incomplete or changed later as the archive grows.
